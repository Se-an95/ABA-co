After Care

The After Care division provides support and resources for backpackers who are transitioning back to their daily lives after a backpacking trip. Our team of trained counselors and therapists offers individual and group counseling services to help backpackers deal with post-trip challenges such as readjustment, re-entry shock, and depression. In addition, we provide educational materials and resources to help backpackers develop coping skills and strategies for managing the challenges of re-entry into daily life.

Mentorship Program

The Mentorship Program division matches experienced backpackers with new or less experienced backpackers who are looking for guidance and support. Our mentors provide advice, tips, and encouragement to help mentees develop their skills and confidence in backpacking, and also serve as role models for responsible and sustainable outdoor practices. Our mentorship program is designed to foster a sense of community among backpackers and to promote the values of stewardship, respect, and safety in the outdoors.
